Career Counselling and Interactive Session

The Mathematics and Computing Society of IIT (BHU) organized its first event "Career Counselling and Interactive Session" on the 4th May 2018. The batch of 2018 shared their experiences about different aspects of college life and guided how to choose career options in the future.

MathematIQ

A maths quiz was conducted on 6th October 2018. We saw enthusiastic participation from all branches. This competitive event was conducted exclusively for freshers. It was held in two rounds, in which they made use of their mathematical and problem-solving skills.

Python Workshop

A workshop on Introduction to Python programming was organized by Mathematics and Computing society. The workshop was held in the Department of Mathematical Sciences and saw enormous participation. This interactive workshop was taken by Gaurav Somani experienced in python programming. It started by giving an introduction about the programming language, the convenience it offers in solving complex problems and its use in the emerging field of Machine Learning. It was followed with the description of various python libraries like numpy, pandas and sympy that offers a wide range of functionality.

Talk on Academic Writing

The reader does not start by knowing what we mean. If our words are ambiguous, our meaning will escape him. With this motto, a talk by Prof. P. K. Panda, on “Significance and Process of Academic Writing’ was conducted by Mathematics and Computing Society. Topics like organizational principles in writing, use of pictorial presentation and formatting were discussed.

Intra-Department Sports Meet

Taking part in sports reduces stress and enhances people's moods. To encourage such physical well being, and help people socialize in the society, MACS successfully hosted an Intra-Department Sports Tournament for cricket, badminton, volleyball and table tennis and witnessed huge participation from all years of Ph.D. and IDD.
