About the Society


The Mathematics and Computing Society of IIT BHU (MACS) is a non-profit student organization,under the patronage of the Department of Mathematical Sciences IIT BHU, with the motto “Divided by years, United by MACS”.
The Society is aimed at promoting research and technical skills among the students by providing them opportunity to elucidate their ideas and thoughts beyond boundaries. We are a learned society consisting of professors, research scholars, undergraduate students and the alumni of the department. The Society is responsible for organizing programs, activities, social events,and seminars for the Postgraduate math community at IIT BHU.

Vision

The vision of the society is to promote study and research in mathematics. It aims to enrich computational thinking, technical and analytical skills among students by actively involving them in both academic and extracurricular activities. It also serves as a platform for unifying research scholars, undergraduates and alumni to facilitate the exchange of knowledge and ideas among them and assist each other.

Background

The Mathematics and Computing So ciety was founded in April 2018 to provide our students with a platform to polish their skills and passions to become great leaders for industry and research. The mission is to create a distinct identity for us. To begin with, we have organized some amateur events while professionalism is yet to be developed. Top-ranked JEE qualifiers join our academic programme; there is no dearth of placement offers, and some of our alumni are successful entrepreneurs. But it is just the end of the beginning. We pray “तमसो मा ज्योतिर्गमय “ and aspire to achieve more and more milestones.
